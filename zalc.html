About ZALC &
Our Department

The Zoologist Association of Loyola College (ZALC) is the student association of the PG & Research Department of Advanced Zoology & Biotechnology, Loyola College (Autonomous), Chennai – 600 034. Founded to nurture scientific curiosity and leadership among students of zoology, ZALC is a vibrant forum that bridges academic excellence with hands-on exploration of the biological world. The Department of Advanced Zoology & Biotechnology at Loyola College is one of the most esteemed life-science departments in South India, offering undergraduate, postgraduate and doctoral programmes. The department is renowned for its cutting-edge research in molecular biology, environmental science, aquatic biology, entomology, and biotechnology. Loyola College (Autonomous), established in 1925 by the Society of Jesus, is a NAAC A+++ accredited institution ranked among India's Top 10 colleges by India Today.

Department Heritage

Established 1948 · 70+ Years of Excellence

The Department of Natural Sciences was started in 1948 at Loyola College. A full-fledged Department of Zoology was established in 1948 offering B.Sc. Zoology. Over six decades, it has grown into the PG & Research Department of Advanced Zoology & Biotechnology — a nationally recognised centre for life-science education and research.

  • Programmes OfferedB.Sc. Advanced Zoology & Biotechnology · M.Sc. Zoology · Ph.D. Zoology & Biotechnology
