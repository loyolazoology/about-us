About This Platform

What is ZEN - DNA?

Born from student initiative and departmental vision, ZEN - DNA was designed as a one-stop digital home for the life science community at Loyola — and has since grown into the department's official online presence.

The Story

From E-Learning Platform to Departmental Home

ZEN - DNA — Zoology E-Learning Network: Departmental Nurturing Academics — was conceived as a digital learning ecosystem for students of the PG & Research Department of Advanced Zoology and Biotechnology at Loyola College, Chennai.

The platform was envisioned to bridge classroom learning with digital resources — offering study materials, research insights, departmental news, faculty profiles and academic guidance in one cohesive online environment rooted in the Jesuit tradition of holistic education.

What began as an innovative student-led e-learning initiative has now been elevated and integrated as the Official Website of the Department — a testament to the quality and vision of its creators.

Our Purpose

The Four Pillars of ZEN - DNA

Every feature and page of this platform is built around four core commitments that define our department's digital identity.

E-Learning

Providing accessible digital resources — study materials, lecture notes, video references and research links — to support UG, PG and Ph.D. students in their academic journey at any time, from anywhere.

Nurturing Community

Building a connected community of students, faculty, researchers and alumni — fostering collaboration, mentorship and a shared passion for life sciences rooted in the Jesuit value of cura personalis.

Academic Showcase

Presenting the department's full academic profile — programmes, faculty expertise, research outputs, publications, achievements and facilities — to prospective students, collaborators and the public.

Excellence & Magis

Embodying the Jesuit call to Magis — always doing more — by using technology to extend the department's reach, visibility and impact beyond the classroom and into the digital world.

A Student Dream, Now Officially Recognised

What began as ZEN - DNA — Zoology E-Learning Network: Departmental Nurturing Academics — a student-led digital initiative to serve the academic community of the PG & Research Department of Advanced Zoology and Biotechnology, has evolved into something far greater.

Following its successful launch on 12 December 2025, inaugurated by Rev. Dr. D. Thomas Alexander SJ himself, the platform demonstrated such quality, vision and utility that it has now been formally integrated as the Official Website of the Department.

This transition is a remarkable achievement — a testament to the creators' dedication, the guidance of Dr. V. Pushpa Rani, and the Loyola College tradition of empowering students to lead with courage, creativity and competence.

Platform Features

What This Website Offers

A comprehensive digital gateway to everything the department has to offer — for students, researchers, faculty and visitors.

Academic Resources

Study materials, syllabi, question banks, lecture notes and reference links for all UG, PG and Ph.D. programmes offered by the department.

Faculty Profiles

Detailed profiles of every faculty member — their qualifications, specialisations, publications, funded projects and contact information.

Research Showcase

Highlighting the department's active research areas, publications, Ph.D. scholars, funded projects and national/international collaborations.

Events & News

Up-to-date information on departmental seminars, workshops, field trips, symposia and other academic events and announcements.

Admissions Info

Programme details, eligibility criteria, fee structure and admission procedures for prospective students joining the department.

Achievements & Rankings

A showcase of the department's accolades — NAAC, DBT Star College, UGC SAP, national rankings and individual faculty recognitions.
